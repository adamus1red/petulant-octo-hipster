DNS

DNS settings are VERY dangerous. This can easily break the entire website!

Website

The A Record for the raw domain and www should point to the IP for strathseds.clubs.geeksoc.org

EMail

MX Records should be the following:

priority 100 > mxb.ovh.net

priority 5 > mx2.ovh.net

priority 1 > mx1.ovh.net

SPF spamfilter should below

spf > v=spf1 include:mx.ovh.net~all

Whois

Whois settings should not be changed unless StrathSEDS has moved labs
