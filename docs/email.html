For accounts that are *@strathseds.org please goto http://mail.strathseds.org, for strathseds@hotmail.co.uk please goto http://hotmail.co.uk/ and for strathseds*@groupspaces.com use your personal login and request manager status from a current manager.

Email Redirects

Email redirects are pointed to the relevent commitiee members *@*.strath.ac.uk DO NOT point them to a personal email except for strathseds@hotmail.co.uk

Redirects that must be changed each year are:
